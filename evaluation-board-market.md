<p><p>Evaluation boards are essential tools for assessing power semiconductor performance, and they primarily fall into three market types: </p><p>1. **IGBT Evaluation Boards**: Designed for testing Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors, these boards enable users to evaluate the efficiency and thermal performance in applications like motor control and renewable energy.</p><p>2. **MOSFET Evaluation Boards**: These focus on Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors, allowing analysis of switching speeds and thermal characteristics in power management and conversion systems.</p><p>3. **Other Markets**: This includes evaluation boards for other semiconductor devices, catering to niche applications and innovative technologies in the power electronics sector.</p></p>
