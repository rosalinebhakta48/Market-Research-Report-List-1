<p><strong>The Flax Yarns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Spinning</li><li>Dry Spinning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flax yarns are produced through wet spinning and dry spinning methods. Wet spinning involves dissolving flax fibers in a chemical solution, extruding them through spinnerets into a coagulating bath, and solidifying them into yarn. This method yields strong, durable yarns ideal for textiles. Dry spinning dissolves fibers in a volatile solvent, extruding them into warm air to evaporate the solvent, creating finer, softer yarns. Both methods cater to diverse market needs, with wet spinning favored for industrial applications and dry spinning for premium, lightweight fabrics.</p></p>
