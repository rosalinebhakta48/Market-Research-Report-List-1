<p><strong>The Spinal Fusion Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transforaminal Lumbar Interbody Fusion (TLIF)</li><li>Anterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (ALIF)</li><li>Posterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (PLIF)</li><li>Posterior Lumbar Fusion (PLF)</li><li>Extreme Lateral Interbody Fusion (XLIF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal fusion market includes various surgical techniques to stabilize the spine and alleviate pain. Transforaminal Lumbar Interbody Fusion (TLIF) involves accessing the spine through the side, while Anterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (ALIF) approaches it from the front. Posterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (PLIF) and Posterior Lumbar Fusion (PLF) target the spine from the back. Extreme Lateral Interbody Fusion (XLIF) accesses the spine laterally. Each technique has unique benefits and applications, catering to different patient needs in spinal surgery.</p></p>
