<p><ul><li>Mineral</li><li>Chemical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Concrete Admixture Construction Chemicals Market is divided into two primary types: mineral and chemical admixtures. Mineral admixtures include supplementary cementitious materials like fly ash, slag, and silica fume, which enhance concrete's durability and reduce permeability. Chemical admixtures, on the other hand, are specifically formulated to modify the properties of fresh or hardened concrete, including water reducers, superplasticizers, and retarders, optimizing workability, setting times, and strength. Both types play crucial roles in improving concrete performance and sustainability.</p></p>
