<p><ul><li>Coherent Beam Combination</li><li>Spectral Beam Combination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beam combiner market is primarily segmented into two types: coherent beam combination and spectral beam combination. Coherent beam combination involves integrating multiple laser beams with high phase alignment, resulting in enhanced power and brightness, often used in applications like high-energy lasers. In contrast, spectral beam combination merges beams of different wavelengths, allowing for flexibility in optical systems and increasing efficiency in applications such as telecommunications and sensing. Both methods enhance performance in diverse optical applications.</p></p>
