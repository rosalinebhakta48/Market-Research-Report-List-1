<p><p>The anti-corrosive light fitting market is primarily categorized into two types: single batten and twin batten lights. Single batten fittings typically house one light source and are ideal for smaller areas or applications requiring less light, providing a sleek, compact solution. In contrast, twin batten fittings accommodate two light sources, offering enhanced illumination and coverage for larger spaces. Both types are designed to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making them suitable for industrial and commercial settings where corrosion resistance is essential.</p></p>
