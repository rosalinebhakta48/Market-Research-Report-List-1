<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Mobile Wallet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open and Semi-Open</li><li>Semi-Closed and Closed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile wallet markets can be categorized into four types: </p><p>1. **Open Market**: These wallets allow users to make transactions across various merchants and withdraw cash, providing maximum flexibility. </p><p>2. **Semi-Open Market**: Users can transact with specific merchants but lack the ability to withdraw cash. </p><p>3. **Semi-Closed Market**: Transactions are limited to certain merchants, and cash withdrawal is not possible. </p><p>4. **Closed Market**: These wallets allow transactions only within a single service provider, offering no cash withdrawal or external transaction options.</p></p>
