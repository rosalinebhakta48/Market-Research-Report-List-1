<p><p>The fluid metering pumps market includes diaphragm metering pumps and plunger metering pumps, both designed for precise fluid control in various applications. Diaphragm metering pumps utilize a flexible diaphragm to create a vacuum and deliver accurate flow rates, making them ideal for corrosive or viscous fluids. In contrast, plunger metering pumps employ a reciprocating plunger mechanism, offering high pressure and volume capabilities, suitable for demanding industrial environments. Both types serve critical roles in sectors such as pharmaceuticals, water treatment, and chemical processing.</p></p>
