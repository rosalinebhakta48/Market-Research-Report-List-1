<p><p>The portable water purifiers market comprises several technologies: </p><p>1. **Reverse Osmosis (RO)** employs a membrane to remove contaminants, providing highly purified water.</p><p>2. **Ion Exchange** systems replace harmful ions with safer ones, effectively reducing hardness and contaminants.</p><p>3. **Filtration** uses physical barriers or chemicals to trap impurities, maintaining essential minerals while improving taste.</p><p>4. **Distillation** involves boiling water and collecting vapor, eliminating contaminants through evaporation. </p><p>These methods cater to diverse consumer needs for clean, safe drinking water in various situations.</p></p>
