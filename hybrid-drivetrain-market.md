<p><p>The hybrid drivetrain market comprises three main types: Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs), Full Hybrid Electric Vehicles (Full HEs), and Mild Hybrid Electric Vehicles (Mild HEs). PHEVs can operate on electric power alone for shorter trips and utilize a combustion engine for longer distances. Full HEs combine both electric and gasoline power, optimizing efficiency and performance. Mild HEs use an electric motor to assist the engine but cannot operate solely on electric power, enhancing fuel economy without a significant battery.</p></p>
