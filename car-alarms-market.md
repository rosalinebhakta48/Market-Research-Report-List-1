<p><p>The car alarm market includes **Passive Car Alarms** and **Active Car Alarms**. Passive alarms automatically activate when the vehicle is turned off and doors are locked, requiring no user input. They provide continuous protection. Active alarms, on the other hand, require manual activation by the user, often via a remote or key fob. Both types deter theft by triggering sirens and alerts, but passive systems offer convenience, while active systems allow user control over when the alarm is armed.</p></p>
