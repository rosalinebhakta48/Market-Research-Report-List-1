<p><p>Non-invasive Positive Pressure Ventilators (NPPVs) for in-home use primarily include Single Level Ventilators and Bilevel Ventilators. Single Level Ventilators deliver a constant air pressure throughout the breathing cycle, aiding patients with conditions like obstructive sleep apnea. In contrast, Bilevel Ventilators provide two pressure settings: a higher pressure during inhalation and a lower pressure during exhalation, offering improved comfort and efficiency for patients with more complex respiratory issues. Both types enhance quality of life and facilitate easier breathing at home.</p></p>
