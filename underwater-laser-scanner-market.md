<p><p>The underwater laser scanner market is categorized into short-range, medium-range, and long-range types based on scanning capabilities. Short-range scanners are designed for close-proximity applications, delivering high-resolution data, ideal for detailed inspections. Medium-range scanners balance resolution and distance, suitable for various underwater surveys and marine research. Long-range scanners cover greater distances, making them effective for extensive mapping and exploration, albeit with lower resolution. Each type serves distinct operational needs in marine surveying, conservation, and underwater engineering.</p></p>
