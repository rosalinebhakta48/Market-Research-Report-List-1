<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The unloader valve market is divided into two primary types: manual and automatic. Manual unloader valves require operator intervention to release pressure from a compressor, making them suitable for applications where user control is essential. In contrast, automatic unloader valves function without manual input, using pressure sensors to detect when to unload and reload the compressor, enhancing efficiency and minimizing downtime. Both types cater to various industrial and commercial needs, reflecting different operational requirements and user preferences.</p></p>
