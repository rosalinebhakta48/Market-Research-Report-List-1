<p><p>The Full Ice Protection System (FIPS) encompasses technologies designed to prevent ice formation on aircraft surfaces. The market consists of two primary types: de-icing systems, which remove ice that has already formed, and anti-icing systems, which prevent ice from accumulating in the first place. De-icing typically involves mechanical methods or heating, while anti-icing employs preventive measures like heated surfaces or chemical applications. Both systems are crucial for maintaining aircraft safety and performance in cold weather conditions.</p></p>
