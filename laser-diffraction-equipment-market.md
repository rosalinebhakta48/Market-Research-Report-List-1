<p><strong>The Laser Diffraction Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Particle Analysis</li><li>Dry Particle Analysis</li><li>Spray Particle Analysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser diffraction equipment is categorized based on the sample state for particle size analysis. Wet particle analysis involves dispersing samples in a liquid medium, ideal for fine powders and emulsions. Dry particle analysis is suited for powders in their solid state, using airflow to disperse the sample. Spray particle analysis focuses on measuring particles in aerosols or sprays, crucial for industries like pharmaceuticals and environmental monitoring. Each method serves specific applications, influencing the selection based on sample properties and desired results.</p></p>
