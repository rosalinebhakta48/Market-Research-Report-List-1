<p><p>Super Grid market types include High Voltage Grid, Extra-High Voltage (EHV) Grid, and Ultrahigh Voltage (UHV) Grid, each representing advancements in electricity transmission capabilities. High Voltage Grids typically operate between 100 kV to 250 kV, suitable for regional power distribution. EHV Grids range from 250 kV to 800 kV, enabling long-distance transmission with reduced losses. UHV Grids exceed 800 kV, facilitating massive power transfers over extensive distances, playing a crucial role in connecting renewable energy sources and enhancing energy security.</p></p>
