<p><strong>The Cored Solder Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>No Clean</li><li>Water Soluble</li><li>Rosin Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cored solder wire is categorized into three main types based on their flux compositions and cleaning requirements. No Clean solder wire contains minimal flux residues that do not require cleaning post-soldering, offering convenience and efficiency for electronics manufacturing. Water Soluble solder wire, designed for easy cleaning, uses a flux that requires removal with water, making it ideal for applications needing high reliability. Rosin Based solder wire utilizes natural resin flux and typically requires cleaning, providing effective soldering with traditional characteristics.</p></p>
