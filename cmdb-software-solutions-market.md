<p><p>CMDB (Configuration Management Database) software solutions are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CMDBs offer flexibility, scalability, and reduced maintenance costs, allowing organizations to access configuration data remotely. These solutions typically feature subscription pricing and automatic updates. On-premises CMDBs, however, provide greater control and security as they reside within an organization's infrastructure. They require upfront investment and ongoing management but are preferred by those with stringent regulatory or compliance requirements that demand localized data handling.</p></p>
