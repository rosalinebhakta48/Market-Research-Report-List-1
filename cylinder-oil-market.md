<p><strong>The Cylinder Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-BN</li><li>Mid-BN</li><li>Low-BN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cylinder oil market is categorized into three distinct types based on the concentration of base number (BN) additives: High-BN, Mid-BN, and Low-BN oils. High-BN oils, containing more than 70 BN, are primarily used for vessels operating in high-sulfur fuel environments and require robust acid neutralization. Mid-BN oils, ranging from 30 to 70 BN, provide a balance for varied operational conditions. Low-BN oils, with fewer than 30 BN, are suited for low-sulfur fuel usage, emphasizing reduced emissions and environmental compliance.</p></p>
