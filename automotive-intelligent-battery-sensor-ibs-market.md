<p><p>The Automotive Intelligent Battery Sensor (IBS) market includes **Passive Sensors** and **Active Sensors**. Active Sensors, on the other hand, provide advanced functionalities such as real-time data analysis, state-of-charge estimation, and communication with vehicle systems, enhancing battery management and efficiency. Both types cater to varying automotive needs, with passive sensors suited for basic applications and active sensors for modern, connected vehicles requiring precise battery monitoring and optimization.</p></p>
