<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ultrasound Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Mode</li><li>B-Mode or 2D Mode</li><li>C-Mode</li><li>M-Mode</li><li>Doppler Mode</li><li>Pulse Inversion Mode</li><li>Harmonic Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasound machines are categorized into various market types based on their imaging techniques. A-Mode provides a one-dimensional view, mainly for measuring distances. B-Mode, or 2D Mode, offers two-dimensional images, widely used in diagnostics. C-Mode focuses on specific tissue planes, while M-Mode captures motion in real-time. Doppler Mode detects blood flow and vessel abnormalities. Pulse Inversion Mode enhances image quality by reducing artifacts, and Harmonic Mode improves resolution by using harmonic frequencies, expanding their applications in medical imaging.</p></p>
