<p><ul><li>Single-Mode Fiber Optic Cables</li><li>Multi-Mode Fiber Optic Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The outdoor fiber optic cables market is primarily divided into two types: single-mode and multi-mode fiber optic cables. Single-mode fibers are designed for long-distance communication, using a single light wavelength to minimize signal loss and maximize distance, making them ideal for telecommunications and large networks. In contrast, multi-mode fibers support multiple light wavelengths, catering to shorter distances and higher bandwidth applications, often used within buildings or local area networks. Each type serves specific needs within the telecommunications infrastructure.</p></p>
