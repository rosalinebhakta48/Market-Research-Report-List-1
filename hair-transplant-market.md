<p><strong>The Hair Transplant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT)</li><li>Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair transplant market primarily consists of two methods: Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). FUT involves removing a strip of scalp to extract hair follicles, which are then transplanted to bald areas. This method typically leaves a linear scar. In contrast, FUE extracts individual hair follicles using a specialized tool, resulting in minimal scarring and quicker recovery. Both techniques cater to varying patient needs and preferences, impacting market trends and demand for hair restoration solutions.</p></p>
