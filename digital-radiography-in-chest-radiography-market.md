<p><p>Digital Radiography (DR) in chest radiography encompasses two primary technologies: Computed Radiography (CR) and Direct Radiography (DR). CR tech uses imaging plates to capture data, which is later digitized for analysis, offering flexibility and lower initial costs. In contrast, DR tech employs flat-panel detectors to provide immediate image results, enhancing workflow efficiency and diagnostic accuracy. Both systems play a vital role in modern thoracic imaging, catering to various clinical needs and improving patient outcomes through better image quality and faster processing times.</p></p>
