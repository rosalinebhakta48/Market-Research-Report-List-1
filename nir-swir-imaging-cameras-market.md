<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NIR-SWIR imaging cameras market comprises two main types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture images in a two-dimensional format, making them ideal for static scenes or applications requiring full-frame imaging. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, which is suitable for high-speed imaging and continuous monitoring, often used in industrial inspection and quality control. Both types leverage near-infrared and short-wave infrared wavelengths for enhanced imaging capabilities in various applications.</p></p>
