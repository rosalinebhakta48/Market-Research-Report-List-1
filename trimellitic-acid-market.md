<p><p>Trimellitic acid (TMA) is an organic compound with the chemical formula C9H6O6, primarily used as a raw material in the production of polymers, plasticizers, and coatings. It is a key intermediate in the manufacture of trimellitate esters, which are widely used as plasticizers in PVC products, offering superior heat resistance and durability. Additionally, TMA is utilized in the production of high-performance polyimide resins, adhesives, and inks, making it a versatile chemical in various industries.</p><p>The Trimellitic Acid Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.7% during the forecast period.
