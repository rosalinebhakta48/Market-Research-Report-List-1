<p><strong>The Transparent ABS Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion Graft Copolymerization</li><li>Bulk Copolymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transparent ABS Resin market is segmented into two primary types based on polymerization methods: Emulsion Graft Copolymerization and Bulk Copolymerization. Emulsion Graft Copolymerization involves the simultaneous polymerization of acrylonitrile and butadiene in an aqueous medium, resulting in good impact resistance and clarity. In contrast, Bulk Copolymerization occurs in a solid form with higher concentrations, providing enhanced thermal and mechanical properties. Both methods cater to different applications, influencing market dynamics and material performance.</p></p>
