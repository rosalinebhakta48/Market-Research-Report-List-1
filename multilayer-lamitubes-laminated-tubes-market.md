<p><p>The multilayer lamitubes market, also known as laminated tubes, comprises two primary types: plastic lamitubes and aluminium lamitubes. Plastic lamitubes are lightweight, flexible, and resistant to moisture, making them suitable for products like cosmetics and pharmaceuticals.
