<p><p>Latent curing agents for epoxy resin are categorized into powder and liquid types, each serving distinct applications. Powder latent curing agents offer benefits like easier handling and longer shelf life, often used in coatings and composites. They activate at elevated temperatures, ensuring controlled curing. Liquid latent curing agents, on the other hand, provide flexibility in formulation and easier mixing with epoxy resins. They are commonly used in adhesives and sealants, where precise control over the curing process is essential for performance.</p></p>
