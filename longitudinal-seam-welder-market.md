<p><p>The longitudinal seam welder market consists of three main types: automatic, manual, and semi-automatic seam welders. Automatic seam welders are fully mechanized, offering high precision and efficiency for large-scale production, reducing labor costs. Manual seam welders require human intervention and are suitable for smaller or custom projects, providing flexibility. Semi-automatic seam welders combine features of both, allowing operators to assist in the welding process while maintaining increased productivity and quality. Each type caters to specific operational needs and production scales.</p></p>
