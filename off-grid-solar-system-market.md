<p><ul><li>Partial Off-grid Solar System</li><li>Completely Off-grid Solar System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The off-grid solar system market is divided into two main types: partial off-grid and completely off-grid systems. Partial off-grid solar systems are designed to supplement traditional power sources, providing renewable energy to reduce grid reliance while still connecting to the grid for backup. Completely off-grid solar systems, on the other hand, operate independently of the power grid. These systems rely solely on solar energy, often incorporating battery storage to ensure a continuous power supply, making them ideal for remote locations.</p></p>
