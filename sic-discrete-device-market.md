<p><p>The SiC discrete device market encompasses three primary types: SiC MOSFETs, SiC diodes, and SiC modules. SiC MOSFETs are crucial for high-efficiency power switching applications, providing low conduction losses and fast switching speeds. SiC diodes, known for their high voltage and thermal stability, excel in rectification applications, enhancing overall system efficiency. SiC modules integrate multiple devices into a single package, optimizing space while improving performance in power conversion systems. Collectively, these devices drive advancements in energy efficiency and thermal management across various industries.</p></p>
