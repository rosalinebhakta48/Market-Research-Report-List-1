<p><p>The vehicle glow plug market is primarily segmented into two types: metal glow plugs and ceramic glow plugs. Metal glow plugs are the traditional option, known for their durability and cost-effectiveness, making them popular in many diesel engines. In contrast, ceramic glow plugs offer faster heating times and improved performance, enhancing engine efficiency, especially in cold starts. Ceramic plugs are often favored in high-performance applications, despite being more expensive. Both types are essential for optimal engine functioning in diesel vehicles.</p></p>
