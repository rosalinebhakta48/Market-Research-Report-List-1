<p><p>The Moving Iron Ammeters market is divided into two primary types: AC Moving Iron Ammeters and DC Moving Iron Ammeters. AC Moving Iron Ammeters measure alternating current, utilizing a movable iron vane that responds to the magnetic field generated by the current flow. In contrast, DC Moving Iron Ammeters measure direct current, operating similarly but calibrated for the unidirectional flow of electricity. Both types are widely used in various applications due to their simplicity, reliability, and cost-effectiveness in current measurement.</p></p>
