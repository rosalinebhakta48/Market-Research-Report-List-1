<p><p>The optical projection screen market comprises various types, including Fresnel screens and black grid screens. Fresnel screens utilize a series of ridges to focus light, providing enhanced brightness and clarity, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor environments. Conversely, black grid screens minimize ambient light reflection, offering superior contrast and deeper blacks, which are vital for professional presentations and cinema experiences. Each type serves unique visual requirements, catering to diverse consumer needs in the projection industry.</p></p>
