<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Scanner</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Singer fingerprint scanner market includes two primary types: capacitive and optical fingerprint scanners. Capacitive fingerprint scanners utilize electrical signals to detect fingerprint patterns by measuring the differences in capacitance created by finger ridges, offering high accuracy and durability. In contrast, optical fingerprint scanners capture images of fingerprints using light, making them generally more affordable but potentially less secure than capacitive types. Both technologies serve various applications in security, mobile devices, and access control, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
