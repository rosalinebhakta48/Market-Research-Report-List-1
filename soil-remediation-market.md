<p><p>The soil remediation market encompasses various methods to restore contaminated land. Bioremediation uses microorganisms to degrade pollutants, while chemical oxidation employs chemical agents to neutralize contaminants. Stabilization/solidification involves binding contaminants to reduce their mobility and toxicity. Soil washing utilizes water and chemical additives to remove contaminants from soil particles. Thermal desorption heats soil to evaporate and capture volatile pollutants. Each method addresses specific contamination challenges, catering to diverse environmental needs and regulatory standards.</p></p>
