<p><ul><li>Surface Magnetic Pole</li><li>Embedded Magnetic Pole</li><li>Annular Magnetic Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brushless DC motor driver market features three main types based on magnetic pole configurations: Surface Magnetic Pole, Embedded Magnetic Pole, and Annular Magnetic Pole. Surface Magnetic Pole motors have magnets positioned on the rotor's surface, providing high torque and efficiency. Embedded Magnetic Pole motors have magnets embedded within the rotor, allowing for compact designs and improved performance. Annular Magnetic Pole motors utilize a ring-shaped magnetic configuration, enhancing power density and thermal management, making each type suitable for different applications and performance requirements.</p></p>
