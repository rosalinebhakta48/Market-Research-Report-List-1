<p><p>The Steel Core Aluminum Stranded Wire (ACSR) market includes several types: ACSR, ACSR/AW, and ACSR/TW. ACSR features a core of steel reinforced by aluminum strands, enhancing strength and conductivity, ideal for overhead power transmission. ACSR/AW incorporates aluminum clad steel for improved corrosion resistance, while ACSR/TW utilizes a larger steel core to increase tensile strength. Each type caters to different applications in electrical distribution and transmission, balancing efficiency, durability, and performance based on specific requirements.</p></p>
