<p><strong>The Electric Waveguide Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rectangular Waveguide Switch</li><li>Double Ridge Waveguide Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric waveguide switches market consists primarily of two types: rectangular waveguide switches and double ridge waveguide switches. Rectangular waveguide switches are designed for high-frequency applications, providing low insertion loss and high isolation due to their rectangular cross-section. In contrast, double ridge waveguide switches feature an enhanced design that offers wider bandwidth and improved power handling capabilities, making them suitable for more demanding environments. Both types serve crucial roles in telecommunications, radar, and microwave systems.</p></p>
