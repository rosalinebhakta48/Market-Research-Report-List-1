<p><ul><li>Single Stage Pump</li><li>Multiple Stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inline process pump market is categorized into single-stage and multiple-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps consist of a single impeller and are designed for lower pressure applications, providing efficient performance in moderate flow systems. In contrast, multiple-stage pumps contain several impellers and are used for higher pressure requirements, making them suitable for applications like water supply and industrial processes. Both types are critical in optimizing fluid transfer in various sectors, including chemical processing, water treatment, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
