<p><p>The lumbar artificial disc market comprises primarily two types: metal and biopolymer. Metal artificial discs often utilize materials like titanium or cobalt-chromium, providing durability and strength for spine stabilization. In contrast, biopolymer discs are made from biologically compatible materials that mimic natural disc properties, promoting better integration with body tissues. Both types aim to alleviate pain and restore function in patients with degenerative disc disease, catering to different patient needs and advancing spinal surgery technology.</p></p>
