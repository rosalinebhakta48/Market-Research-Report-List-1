<p><strong>The Recovery Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soda Recovery Boiler</li><li>Kraft Recovery Boiler</li><li>Chemical Recovery Boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recovery boiler market comprises three primary types: soda recovery boilers, kraft recovery boilers, and chemical recovery boilers. Soda recovery boilers are used in the production of sodium carbonate, recovering chemicals from the pulping process. Kraft recovery boilers focus on recovering chemicals from wood pulp production, essential for the kraft process in paper manufacturing. Chemical recovery boilers enhance efficiency by reprocessing various chemicals from different industrial operations, playing a crucial role in sustainability and resource management in the pulp and paper industry.</p></p>
