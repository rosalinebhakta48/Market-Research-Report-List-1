<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal</li><li>Thermal Wax Transfer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thermal Transfer Overprinting (TTO) printers market is primarily divided into two types: Direct Thermal and Thermal Wax Transfer. Direct Thermal printers utilize heat-sensitive media, producing images or text without ink, ideal for short-term labels and packaging. Conversely, Thermal Wax Transfer printers use a ribbon coated with wax or resin, allowing for more durable prints on various substrates, suitable for longer-lasting applications. Both technologies cater to diverse industries, enhancing efficiency and print quality in labeling and packaging processes.</p></p>
