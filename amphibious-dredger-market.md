<p><p>The amphibious dredger market includes various types designed for different dredging applications. Cutter-suction dredgers utilize a rotating cutter head to loosen material before suctioning it into a pump, making them effective for tough substrates. Trailing suction hopper dredgers scoop material from the seabed using a trailing pipe, storing it in hoppers for transport, ideal for large-scale projects. Other types encompass specialized dredgers for unique environments or tasks, such as environmental dredging, maintaining waterways, and land reclamation efforts.</p></p>
