<p><p>The junction box market consists of two main types: non-metallic and metallic junction boxes. Non-metallic junction boxes are typically made from materials such as PVC or fiberglass, offering lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and non-conductive properties suited for indoor and outdoor applications. In contrast, metallic junction boxes are constructed from materials like steel or aluminum, providing enhanced durability, protection against physical damage, and fire resistance, making them ideal for industrial settings. Both types serve crucial roles in electrical safety and organization.</p></p>
