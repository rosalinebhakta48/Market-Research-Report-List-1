<p><ul><li>Visibility Tint</li><li>Enhancement Tint</li><li>Opaque Tint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The color contact lenses market features three main types: visibility tint, enhancement tint, and opaque tint. Visibility tint lenses have a light tint that helps wearers see and handle them easily, while enhancement tint lenses add depth to natural eye color, making it more vibrant without completely changing it. Opaque tint lenses are designed to completely alter the eye color, providing a bold look, often used for cosmetic purposes or special effects. Each type caters to different aesthetic and functional needs.</p></p>
