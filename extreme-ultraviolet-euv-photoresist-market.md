<p><p>The Extreme Ultraviolet (EUV) photoresist market is categorized into two main types: dry photoresist and liquid photoresist. Dry photoresist, often applied as a film, offers advantages such as uniform thickness and ease of handling, making it suitable for high-resolution applications. Liquid photoresist, on the other hand, is applied as a solution, allowing for greater flexibility in coating and complex patterning. Both types play critical roles in advanced semiconductor manufacturing processes, particularly in producing intricate microcircuit designs.</p></p>
