<p><p>The robotic sandblasting systems market features three main types: Gantry type, wall-mounted, and adsorption type. Gantry-type systems operate on a rail system, allowing for extensive movement and versatility in larger areas. Wall-mounted systems are designed for efficient space utilization, providing a fixed, streamlined approach for smaller applications. Adsorption type systems focus on environmentally-friendly practices by capturing and recycling abrasive materials during the blasting process, reducing waste and promoting sustainability in various industrial settings.</p></p>
