<p><p>Multimodal transportation for the chemical and petroleum market involves the integration of various transport modes to optimize the delivery of goods. Vessel Operating MTOs (VO-MTOs) own and operate their vessels, managing cargo transportation across waterways efficiently while adhering to safety regulations. In contrast, Non-Vessel Operating MTOs (NVO-MTOs) do not own vessels but coordinate logistics, booking cargo with shipping lines and ensuring seamless transfers between different transport modes. Both play crucial roles in ensuring timely and secure transport within this specialized market.</p></p>
