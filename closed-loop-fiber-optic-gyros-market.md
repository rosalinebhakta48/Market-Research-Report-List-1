<p><p>The closed-loop fiber optic gyroscope (FOG) market is categorized into single-axis and multi-axis systems. Single-axis gyros measure rotational movement along one axis, making them ideal for simpler applications like automotive and handheld navigation. In contrast, multi-axis gyros can monitor motion across multiple axes, offering enhanced accuracy and data richness, suitable for complex systems like aerospace, robotics, and marine navigation. The choice between these types depends on the application's requirements for precision, complexity, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
