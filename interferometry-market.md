<p><p>The interferometry market encompasses various technologies used for precision measurement and analysis. Laser Fizeau Interferometers are ideal for surface testing, while Mach-Zehnder Interferometers excel in optical path difference analysis. Michelson Interferometers are widely used in spectroscopy and metrology. Fabry-Perot Interferometers are known for high resolution in spectral analysis. Sagnac Interferometers are utilized for detecting rotation or inertial navigation. Twyman-Green Laser Interferometers specialize in measuring surface irregularities and wavefront distortions, serving critical roles in scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>
