<p><p>The Heavy-Duty Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging Infrastructure market includes DC and AC chargers. AC chargers deliver slower charging, converting power within the vehicle, suitable for overnight or extended stops. Both types cater to heavy-duty EVs, with DC chargers focusing on efficiency and speed, while AC chargers emphasize cost-effectiveness and widespread compatibility for diverse charging scenarios.</p></p>
