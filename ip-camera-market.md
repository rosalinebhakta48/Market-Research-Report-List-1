<p><p>The IP camera market is classified into two main types: centralized and decentralized. Centralized IP cameras rely on a central server for data storage and processing, providing easier management and integration within extensive surveillance systems. In contrast, decentralized IP cameras operate independently, with each unit capable of local recording and processing, enhancing resilience and reducing bandwidth usage. Both types cater to different security needs, offering flexibility in deployment based on organizational requirements and infrastructure.</p></p>
