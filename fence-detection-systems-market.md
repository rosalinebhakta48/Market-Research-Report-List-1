<p><p>Fence Detection Systems are primarily categorized into two market types: Fence Mounted Intrusion Detection and Wall Mounted Intrusion Detection. Fence Mounted Intrusion Detection systems are installed on perimeters such as fences, using sensors to detect vibrations or disturbances, thereby enhancing security in outdoor areas. In contrast, Wall Mounted Intrusion Detection systems are affixed to vertical surfaces, providing monitoring and alerting capabilities for intrusions against building walls, helping secure commercial and residential properties from unauthorized access.</p></p>
