<p><strong>The Reactive Silencer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resonant Silencer</li><li>Expansion Silencer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reactive silencer market primarily encompasses resonant silencers and expansion silencers, both designed to reduce noise in industrial applications. Resonant silencers utilize specific frequency absorption techniques, where internal structures resonate at targeted sound frequencies, effectively cancelling out undesirable noise. In contrast, expansion silencers work by allowing gases to expand and slow down, minimizing sound pressure levels through increased volume. Both types are crucial in ensuring compliance with environmental regulations while enhancing operational efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
