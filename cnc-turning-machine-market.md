<p><strong>The CNC Turning Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spindle Orientation: Horizontal</li><li>Spindle Orientation: Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CNC turning machines can be categorized based on spindle orientation into horizontal and vertical types. In horizontal spindle CNC turning, the spindle is positioned parallel to the floor, allowing for efficient chip removal and easy access to tooling, making it suitable for larger workpieces. Conversely, vertical spindle CNC turning machines feature a vertically aligned spindle, which is ideal for shorter and heavier components. Each orientation offers unique advantages depending on workpiece shape, size, and production requirements, influencing market demand and applications.</p></p>
