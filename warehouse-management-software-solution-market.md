<p><p>Warehouse Management Software (WMS) solutions can be categorized into two primary market types: Cloud-based and On-Premise. Cloud-based WMS solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT costs. They allow for real-time updates and remote access. On-Premise WMS, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company's hardware, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves distinct business needs and preferences.</p></p>
