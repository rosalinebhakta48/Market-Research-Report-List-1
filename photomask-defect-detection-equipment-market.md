<p><ul><li>Photomask Substrate Inspection Equipment</li><li>Photomask Pattern Detection Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photomask defect detection equipment market comprises two main types: photomask substrate inspection equipment and photomask pattern detection equipment. Photomask substrate inspection equipment focuses on detecting defects on the photomask's blank substrate, ensuring material integrity before patterning. In contrast, photomask pattern detection equipment examines the patterned surface of the photomask for anomalies or defects that could affect the final product's quality. Both types are crucial for maintaining the precision and reliability of photolithography processes in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
