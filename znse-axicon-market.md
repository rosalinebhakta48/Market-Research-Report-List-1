<p><p>The ZnSe Axicon market is segmented based on axicon angles, influencing optical applications. Axicons with angles less than 5° are ideal for producing shallow depth-of-focus beams, suitable for precision tasks. The 5° to 10° range enhances depth while maintaining beam quality, useful in laser machining. Angles from 10° to 20° create pronounced focusing effects, beneficial for imaging systems. Axicons with angles between 20° and 25° offer significant beam divergence, primarily used in applications like laser cutting and material processing.</p></p>
