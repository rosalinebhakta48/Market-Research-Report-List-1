<p><ul><li>Squirrel-cage</li><li>Slip-ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-voltage asynchronous motor market is primarily segmented into two types: squirrel-cage and slip-ring motors. Squirrel-cage motors are characterized by their simple design, reliability, and low maintenance, making them widely used in various industrial applications. In contrast, slip-ring motors feature external windings connected to a slip ring, allowing for better control of speed and starting torque, which is advantageous in heavy-duty applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs, catering to diverse industrial requirements.</p></p>
