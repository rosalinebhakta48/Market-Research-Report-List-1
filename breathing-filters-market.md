<p><ul><li>Mechanical Filter</li><li>Electrostatic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The breathing filters market is primarily categorized into two main types: mechanical filters and electrostatic filters. Mechanical filters utilize physical barriers, such as fine membranes, to trap particles and pollutants, providing effective filtration for airborne contaminants. In contrast, electrostatic filters leverage electrical charges to attract and capture particulates, enhancing filtrations efficiency. Both types are essential for improving air quality in various settings, such as hospitals, industrial environments, and residential spaces, helping to protect respiratory health and enhance comfort.</p></p>
