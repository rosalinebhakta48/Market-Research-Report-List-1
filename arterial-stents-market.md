<p><p>The arterial stents market is primarily divided into coronary and peripheral stents. Coronary stents are utilized to treat narrowing or blockages in the coronary arteries, enhancing blood flow to the heart and reducing the risk of heart attacks. Peripheral stents are designed for treating arterial blockages in other parts of the body, such as the legs or pelvis, helping to restore circulation. Both types are pivotal in managing cardiovascular diseases and improving patient outcomes through minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
