<p><strong>The Integrated Touchscreen Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Cell</li><li>In-Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Integrated Touchscreen Module market is primarily divided into two types: On-Cell and In-Cell technologies. On-Cell touchscreens incorporate touch sensors on top of the display layer, allowing for thinner designs but potentially affecting display quality. In-Cell touchscreens integrate touch sensors within the display itself, resulting in even slimmer profiles and improved sensitivity. Both technologies cater to demands for sleek devices in consumer electronics, enhancing user interaction without compromising visual performance.</p></p>
