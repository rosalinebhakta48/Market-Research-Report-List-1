<p><strong>The Flame Retardant Polyester Fibre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phosphorous Flame Retardant</li><li>Halogen Flame Retardant</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flame Retardant Polyester Fibre market is categorized into three primary types based on their chemical compositions. Phosphorous flame retardants utilize phosphorus compounds to inhibit flammability by forming protective char layers during combustion. Halogen flame retardants, derived from bromine or chlorine, effectively disrupt the combustion process but can produce toxic byproducts. The "Other" category encompasses various alternative flame retardant technologies, including mineral-based and intumescent systems, which aim to enhance fire resistance while minimizing environmental and health impacts.</p></p>
