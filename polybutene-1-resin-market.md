<p><p>The Polybutene-1 (Resin) market is primarily divided into two types: Homopolymer Type and Random Copolymer Type. Homopolymer Type consists of long polymer chains made solely of butene-1, offering excellent mechanical properties and thermal stability, making it suitable for rigid applications. Random Copolymer Type, on the other hand, incorporates different monomers, providing enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, ideal for diverse applications like flexible packaging and piping systems. Both types cater to distinct industry needs, driving market growth.</p></p>
