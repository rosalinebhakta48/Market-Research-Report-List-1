<p><p>Wriggle pumps encompass tube and hose pumps, integral in various industries for fluid handling. Tube pumps utilize flexible tubing to create a peristaltic action, offering precise flow control and minimizing contamination risk. Hose pumps, on the other hand, operate by compressing a hose to move fluids, ideal for abrasive or viscous materials due to their robust construction. Both types are valued for their reliability, low maintenance needs, and versatility, making them essential in sectors like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and wastewater management.</p></p>
