<p><p>The image sensor market primarily includes three types: CMOS, CCD, and others. CMOS image sensors, known for their low power consumption and faster processing speeds, dominate due to their integration capabilities in smartphones and digital cameras. CCD sensors, while offering superior image quality and low noise levels, are typically more expensive and power-hungry, making them suitable for specialized applications like astrophotography and high-end video cameras. The "other" category encompasses emerging technologies such as organic and hybrid sensors, enhancing imaging capabilities across various industries.</p></p>
