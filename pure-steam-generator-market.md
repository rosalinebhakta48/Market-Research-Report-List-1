<p><ul><li>Re-boiler Generator</li><li>Thermosyphon Generator/ External Rising Film Evaporator</li><li>Falling Film Evaporator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pure Steam Generator market includes several types: Re-boiler Generators, which use waste heat to produce steam efficiently; Thermosyphon Generators or External Rising Film Evaporators, which utilize natural circulation to create steam from water without mechanical pumps; and Falling Film Evaporators, where a thin film of liquid flows down vertical tubes, enhancing heat transfer and evaporation rates. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, application, and operational cost, catering to diverse industrial needs in sterilization and pharmaceutical processes.</p></p>
