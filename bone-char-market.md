<p><ul><li>Granules</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bone Char market includes two primary types: Granules and Powder. Granules are coarse, porous particles used mainly in water filtration, sugar refining, and metal purification due to their high adsorption capacity. Powder, on the other hand, is finely ground bone char, often utilized in agriculture as a soil amendment or in industrial applications requiring finer particle sizes. Both forms are derived from animal bones, processed through pyrolysis, and valued for their carbon-rich, porous structure.</p></p>
