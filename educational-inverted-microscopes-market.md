<p><ul><li>Monocular</li><li>Binocular</li><li>Trinocular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The educational inverted microscopes market is categorized into three types based on optical configurations: monocular, binocular, and trinocular. Monocular microscopes feature a single eyepiece, ideal for basic viewing tasks. Binocular models, with two eyepieces, enhance comfort and depth perception, making them suitable for prolonged use. Trinocular microscopes include an additional optical path for cameras, allowing simultaneous viewing and imaging; this is beneficial for educational settings that require documentation and analysis of specimens. Each type serves distinct educational needs and preferences.</p></p>
